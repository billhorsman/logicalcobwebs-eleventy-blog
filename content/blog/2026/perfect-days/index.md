Koji Yakusho has such an interesting face and charm that watching his daily routine is absolutely mesmorising. Everything done with such care and focus. If this was an hour longer and they filled in that time just with more of him going about his daily life then I would be totally onboard.  Straight into my [top 100](/bill/films/perfect-days-2023/), at the expense of North by Northwest (sorry Mr. Hitchcock, it's a cut-throat business being in the top 100).
